Career Profile

Zhenyu Ming is a researcher of Huawei Theory Lab, devoted to network recovery, clock synchronization and other optimization modelling tasks in ICT. He received his doctoral degree (Jan 2022, Supervisor: Associated Prof. Liping Zhang) and bachelor degree (Jun 2017) in Mathematics at Tsinghua University (THU), China. He is a research assistant of Prof. Liqun Qi at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (Hong Kong) during Jul. 2018 - Oct. 2018.

He conducts research in matrix optimization, tensor computation and related algorithms and theories in ICT field.
